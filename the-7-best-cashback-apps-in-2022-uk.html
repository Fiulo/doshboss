The 7 Best Cashback Apps in 2022 (UK)

Fiulo February 14, 2022

Do you often shop online?

Cashback apps are an amazing way to save money and earn rewards from your usual shopping habits.

These handy mobile apps help you find great deals and cash in on many of those purchases you typically make, with rewards ranging from pennies to £100s of pounds. 

Not only does this save you plenty of time and effort, you will also start to build up a decent sum as you use these apps more and more.

In order to help you, I've put together this guide on some of the most popular and trustworthy cashback apps available right now. You can then use these rewards towards your next shopping trip or even a vacation!

For more info on how cashback apps work, check out the following links:

Top Cashback

https://www.topcashback.co.uk/

Top Cashback is one of the oldest and best known cashback apps in the market. It supports thousands of popular retailers and can save you £100s a year.

All you need to do is sign up for free and select an offer. Complete the purchase and your account will be credited. It's that simple.

The app is available for iPhone, Android, and Web.

Shopmium

Shopmium is another popular cashback app that offers cashback on purchases made with your credit or debit card at a lhuge selection of stores across the world, whether online or in store.
It's also one of the most powerful too, as it includes a comprehensive catalogue of merchants, and the ability to claim up to 100% cashback from nearby merchants. 
To claim cashback, just download the Shopmium app on your phone and add your chosen store(s) to your shopping list. Once you complete your purchase, Shopmium will tally up the money you've earned and send you a payouts.

Quidco

Get cashback when you shop at popular stores like Nike, Amazon, Target, Walmart and hundreds more. If you’re not already a Quidco member, it’s quick and easy to join. 
There's no catch, so why not give it a go?
Visit one of the Quidco supported brands, click on the cashback link or code and earn cashback after you complete your purchase.
One major bonus of Quidco is that it will even give you extra if you can find a better cashback rate elsewhere.

Honey

Honey is a well-known coupon finding app. However, you can also earn rewards through Honey Gold.
Honey Gold helps you collect points on purchases just like cash.
How does that work? You redeem your points for gift cards -- or even cash -- from brands like Amazon, eBay, Kohl's, Best Buy, Staples, Macy's, and others. If you're a frequent shopper, tt's like being given a  gift card for free!

BeFrugal

BeFrugal offers a huge database of participating retailers and some of the best prices you can find.

The rates vary based on the kind of deal, the price of the item and whether or not you take advantage of their weekly discounts or promo codes. Be sure to sign up for BeFrugal’s free emails, where they send out exclusive coupons that you can’t find anywhere else.


Curve

Curve allows you to link your debit or credit card in order to earn rewards on your shopping.

Curve is a fantastic way to earn while you shop. The company has a clear focus on providing its members with top-notch value for money and a fun and engaging way to make money on the things they’re buying. And that’s just the start.

Additionally, Curve allows you to apply for a debit card with its service that gives your cashback when you pay in-store and includes other features such as increased buyer protection and cheaper foreign exchange rates.

Swagbucks

Swagbucks is mainly known as a survey site. However, it also allows you to earn points on popular shopping sites and through special offers. You can then redeem those points for cash or gift cards.

Registration takes only a few minutes and is free. There is no minimum level of spending to start earning points.  You will even get free points for signing up with the provided link.

Crypto.com

This app is different to the rest as it offers cashback in the form of cryptocurrency using its own CRO token.  It is similar to a cash back card, but the process is a little different. Cashback is awarded instantly when any eligible purchase is made using their provided card.
Its free to sign up and the card can be used in any location that accepts MasterCard and can be used to withdraw cash at ATMs.
